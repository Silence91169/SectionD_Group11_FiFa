## Slide 8 — Methodology: Statistical Validation

| Test | What It Measures | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Mann-Whitney U Test | Starters vs. Substitutes performance | Significant gap confirmed · p < 0.05 |
| Chi-Square Test | Captain impact on match events | Captains involved in disproportionate key events |
| Pearson Correlation | Goals & Discipline correlation | Goals and high-discipline play positively correlated |

**Overall Conclusion:** Player roles (starter, substitute, captain) are measurable, statistically significant performance multipliers.
